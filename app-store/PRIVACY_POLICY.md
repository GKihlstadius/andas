# ANDAS Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026

## TL;DR

- All your data stays on your device
- We don't collect personal information
- We don't use analytics that track behavior
- We don't share data with third parties
- You can delete your data at any time

## Data We Collect

**None.**

ANDAS stores all data locally on your device using your phone's built-in storage (AsyncStorage). This includes:
- Your preferences and settings
- Your session history
- Your feedback

## Data We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage patterns
- Behavioral analytics
- Crash logs (unless you explicitly opt in)

## How We Use Data

The data stored on your device is used to:
- Personalize your breathing exercises
- Track your progress (for your eyes only)
- Adapt exercises based on your feedback

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We don't have access to it.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:
1. Go to Settings in the app
2. Tap "Reset All Data"
3. Confirm deletion

## Children's Privacy

ANDAS is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we'll notify you in the app.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:
privacy@andas.app
